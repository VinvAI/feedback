# Code of Conduct

This is a public space for the vinv community. To keep it useful and welcoming for everyone, we ask that you:

- **Be respectful.** Treat others with kindness. No harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- **Stay on topic.** Keep issues focused on bugs, feature requests, and support for vinv.
- **Be constructive.** Explain problems clearly and assume good intent.
- **Don't spam.** No advertising, off-topic self-promotion, or duplicate issues.
- **Protect privacy.** Don't post other people's personal information, and avoid sharing your own sensitive data (credentials, tokens, private keys) in public issues.

Maintainers may edit, lock, or close issues and comments that violate these guidelines, and may block repeat offenders.

For sensitive concerns, contact us at **support@vinv.ai**.
